# Privacy Policy for ТСН АвтоРеклама

## Data Collection
This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or audio content to external servers. All audio analysis and processing are performed locally within the user's browser.

## Data Usage
The extension only accesses the YouTube Studio editor page to automate the placement of ad breaks based on the visual waveform provided by YouTube.

## Third-Party Access
No data is shared with third parties.

## Changes
We reserve the right to update this policy at any time.
